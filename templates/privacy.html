Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use the service, we also store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate spending summaries, and keep your account secure. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your associated data is removed from our systems. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party providers for functions such as hosting and infrastructure. These providers only process data as necessary to deliver their service to us and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, or if you'd like to request deletion of your account and data, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
